# Discover Ruston, Louisiana: A Family Getaway Destination Are you looking for a fun and exciting family getaway? Look no further than Ruston, Louisiana! Located in the heart of Louisiana, Ruston is a charming town that's packed with activities and attractions that the whole family will love. In this guide, we'll highlight some of the top things to do in Ruston, and we'll also tell you why you should make a stop at A&H Games, the local game store. ## Top Attractions in Ruston * **Lincoln Parish Park** This beautiful park is the perfect place for a family picnic or a relaxing day in nature. With miles of hiking and biking trails, a scenic lake, and plenty of space to play, Lincoln Parish Park is a must-visit attraction in Ruston. * **Louisiana Military Museum** If you're interested in history, be sure to visit the Louisiana Military Museum. This museum features exhibits on the state's military history, from the Civil War to modern times. You'll see artifacts and memorabilia from Louisiana's soldiers and learn about their contributions to the country. * **The Dixie Center for the Arts** For a night out, head to The Dixie Center for the Arts. This historic theater hosts a variety of performances, from plays and musicals to concerts and comedy shows. Check the schedule to see what's playing during your visit. ## Fun Things to Do in Ruston * **A&H Games** No family vacation is complete without a trip to the local game store, and A&H Games is Ruston's premier destination for gamers. Browse their selection of board games, card games, and more, and pick up a few fun activities to enjoy during your stay in Ruston. * **Escape Room** Put your problem-solving skills to the test at the Ruston Escape Room. Choose from a variety of themed rooms and work together with your family to solve puzzles and escape before time runs out. * **Louisiana Peach Festival** If you're visiting Ruston in June, be sure to check out the Louisiana Peach Festival. This annual festival celebrates the state's peach industry with live music, food, crafts, and plenty of peach-themed activities for the whole family. ## Where to Stay in Ruston Ruston offers a variety of lodging options to choose from. Here are a few of our favorites: * **The Courtyard by Marriott Ruston** This modern hotel is located in the heart of Ruston, just minutes away from many of the town's top attractions. The hotel features comfortable rooms with all the amenities you need for a relaxing stay, including free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and mini fridges. There's also an outdoor pool and a fitness center on-site. * **The Sleep Inn & Suites** For budget-conscious travelers, the Sleep Inn & Suites is a great option. This affordable hotel offers clean and comfortable rooms with all the basics, including free Wi-Fi and cable TV. There's also a fitness center and a complimentary breakfast buffet each morning. * **The Louisiana Tech University Hotel** If you're visiting Ruston to check out Louisiana Tech University, why not stay on campus? The Louisiana Tech University Hotel offers comfortable and convenient accommodations just steps away from the university's campus. The hotel features spacious rooms with all the amenities you need for a comfortable stay, including free Wi-Fi, cable TV, and mini fridges. ## Plan Your Trip to Ruston Today! With so many attractions and activities to enjoy, Ruston, Louisiana is the perfect destination for a family getaway. Don't forget to stop by A&H Games to pick up some fun games and activities to enjoy during your stay, and be sure to book your stay at one of the town's top hotels. We hope you have a great time exploring Ruston!

Louisiana Military Museum

If you're interested in history, be sure to visit the Louisiana Military Museum. This museum features exhibits on the state's military history, from the Civil War to modern times. You'll see artifacts and memorabilia from Louisiana's soldiers and learn about their contributions to the country.

The Dixie Center for the Arts

The Dixie Center for the Arts is a historic theater that hosts a variety of performances, including plays, musicals, and concerts. The theater is also home to the Piney Hills Harmony Chorus and the North Central Louisiana Arts Council.

A&H Games

A&H Games is a great place to spend an afternoon. This game store has a wide selection of board games, card games, and role-playing games. You can also find collectible card games and miniatures. The store hosts regular game nights and tournaments, so be sure to check the schedule.

The Louisiana Peach Festival

The Louisiana Peach Festival is a fun event that takes place every June. The festival features a parade, live music, a car show, and a peach-eating contest. You can also enjoy a variety of delicious peach dishes, including peach ice cream, peach cobbler, and peach pie.
